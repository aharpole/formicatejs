A cute little plugin that turns spans into inputs.

What's it do?

Hover over the text. Click on it. Edit the text. Click out.

Please Hover!
Click me!
Edit me!
Me too!

Cuuuuuute!

Yes. Is it easy to add? The answer is yes.

 <form id="your_form_object">
    <span class="waiting">Please Hover!</span>
    <span class="waiting">Click me!</span>
    <span class="waiting">Edit me!</span>
    <span class="waiting">Me too!</span>
  </form>

Is this exciting, or what?!
